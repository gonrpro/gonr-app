## Best For (Shop Type / Specialty)

### R.R. Street & Co.
**Best for:** Any US-based professional dry cleaner who wants the widest product selection from the most established supplier. Particularly strong for shops running hydrocarbon (DF-2000™) systems, operations needing multilingual training (English, Spanish, Korean), and cleaners who want one distributor relationship to cover spotting, detergents, pre-treatment, and wet cleaning. The safe, default choice.

### Kreussler Inc.
**Best for:** High-end cleaners processing couture, wedding gowns, silk, cashmere, and leather. Shops marketing 'eco-friendly' cleaning (SolvonK4 is the only bio-based solvent). Operations that have invested in SYSTEMK4 equipment. Cleaners handling museum textiles or irreplaceable items. The quality-above-all choice.

### A.L. Wilson Chemical Co.
**Best for:** Every dry cleaner's spotting board. A.L. Wilson products are not an alternative to other companies — they're a complement. Their spotting agents (QwikGo®, BonGo®, TarGo®, InkGo®, RustGo®) are the baseline setup that other companies' products supplement. The industry-standard spotting choice.

### Adco (Laidlaw)
**Best for:** Budget-conscious operators who want functional spotting coverage without premium pricing. Shops already in the R.R. Street distributor ecosystem. Operations handling mostly standard fabrics (not silk/wool-heavy). The value choice.

### Pariser Industries
**Best for:** Operators who value on-site technical consultation for complex or recurring stain problems. Multi-service operations running both dry cleaning and commercial laundry. Shops in the northeast US. Cleaners who need a premium NSD (Neutra-Lube). The technical-support choice.

### Seitz Inc.
**Best for:** Shops that want the highest non-perc cleaning performance (SENSENE™ KB 161). Operations evaluating solvent transitions — Seitz covers hydrocarbon, SENSENE™, silicone, and enhanced-safety solvents. European-quality-standard cleaners. The maximum-performance choice.

### Royaltone
**Best for:** Any dry cleaner who processes leather, suede, or fur — there is no real alternative. Shops wanting to add leather cleaning as a profitable specialty service. Operations with high silk volume (Silk Magic). The leather-and-specialty choice.

### Büfa Cleaning
**Best for:** European operators (primary market). Shops wanting a single-supplier German system. Cleaners who process leather and need conditioning products. Operations looking for fluorocarbon-reduced finishing. The European-system choice.

### GreenEarth Cleaning
**Best for:** Dry cleaners marketing to environmentally conscious consumers. Shops in markets that prohibit perc. Operations in buildings with strict VOC regulations. Franchise-style businesses that benefit from the GreenEarth brand. The green-marketing choice. Note: supplemental spotting agents from other suppliers (R.R. Street, A.L. Wilson) are required.

### National Chemical Laboratories
**Best for:** NOT a dry cleaning chemical supplier. Relevant only for: commercial carpet stain removal, institutional laundry, and facility maintenance in non-garment areas of a dry cleaning shop. The institutional-cleaning choice.

## Starter Board Recommendations

### Budget Board (~$200-300 investment)
For a new cleaner or small-volume shop:
- **NSD:** Adco Neutra or Pariser Neutra-Lube
- **Protein:** A.L. Wilson QwikGo®
- **Tannin:** A.L. Wilson BonGo®
- **POG:** A.L. Wilson TarGo®
- **Rust:** A.L. Wilson RustGo®
- **Spray pre-spotter:** R.R. Street MultiSPOT® or A.L. Wilson SpotsGo®

### Professional Board (~$500-800 investment)
For a full-service operation:
- Everything in the Budget Board, plus:
- **Enzyme spotter:** A.L. Wilson SoGo "1"® (NOT for silk/wool)
- **Ink remover:** A.L. Wilson InkGo®
- **Tannin (advanced):** R.R. Street General Formula No. 209®
- **Leveling agent:** Royaltone Silk Magic or R.R. Street MultiSPOT®
- **Reducing bleach:** A.L. Wilson ColorGo®
- **Oxidizing bleach:** A.L. Wilson PermaGo®
- **Lipstick:** A.L. Wilson ReddyGo®

### Premium Board (~$1,000-1,500 investment)
For a high-end operation processing delicates, couture, leather:
- Everything in the Professional Board, plus:
- **PRENETT ABC system:** Kreussler (color-coded pre-spotting)
- **DEPRIT Professional kit:** Kreussler (six-product post-spotting)
- **Silk Magic:** Royaltone (water ring removal)
- **Leather detergent:** Royaltone Leather Wet Cleaning Detergent
- **NSD (premium):** Pariser Neutra-Lube
- **Wet cleaning:** Kreussler LANADOL AKTIV + LANADOL APRET
